I am a BA Product Design graduate (1st Class Honours) at Central Saint Martins, part of UAL, world’s 6th art and design university, according to QS World University Rankings.

I love making magic – experiences, interactions, sudden discoveries and emotions. For this portfolio, I have selected 7 projects done over the past 4 years. Some of them are fully analog, others are full of electric components and machine code, but central to all of the designs is the user that I want to excite.
