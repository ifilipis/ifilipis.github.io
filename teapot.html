Teapot by Philipp P
This project is a physical part of Orenburg branding that I developed in collaboration with the local government.
The brand
Orenburg region is one of the Russian territorial units.

Located on the geographical border between Europe and Asia, Orenburg serves a connection between the two worlds and cultures. The logo represents a variety of symbols of Orenburg land. Wild flowers, cultural diversity, geographical position, natural gas and natural landscapes.
One of the key elements of the brand is a tulip leaf. We have decided to continue the brand with a line of distinct and recognizeable products. And teapot is one of them. It inherits the brand's design language and spirit.

It required some engineering, in order to figure out the manufactureable solution to let the liquid move inside the teapot, as it doesn't have a spout.
